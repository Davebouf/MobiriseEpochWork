Who is Dean Wilkinson?

Epoch Work founder & CEO, Dean Wilkinson, is a self-made blue collar entrepreneur who built, bought and sold several multi-million dollar home service companies. Despite his success, he realized that something was missing. His day-to-day wasn’t aligned with his beliefs and he was missing the ever-important feelings of fulfillment, satisfaction, and growth. He needed a change.

So began a six-year intellectual and spiritual journey, immersed in reflection of his own experiences and the teachings and writings of spiritual mentors, thought leaders, and influencers. What emerged was The Epoch Way - a unique method of empowering entrepreneurs to evoke their highest potential and purpose by doing the work they are meant to do with employees and customers they love working with, all while maximizing peace, freedom, and prosperity.

Now, he enjoys traveling the country, coaching entrepreneurs and their teams on how to create the epic lives and business they deserve. Dean knows it because he’s lived it...and believes you can to. ARE YOU READY TO LIVE LIKE IT'S TRUE?
