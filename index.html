About Our Community

History    +    About Us

Cornerstone Bible Church of Fort Ann, NY was founded in 1978 in order to help people find "new life" through a relationship with Jesus Christ. That desire continues to be the "cornerstone" of our church today .


While there is great benefit from regularly gathering together in tangible ways, the Church Jesus is building is so much more than a building to worship in, an event to attend, a structured program, or a group to join. Genuine community doesn't just happen, and can take a lot of time and energy. Yet we want to help people connect with Jesus and others and experience the joy of relationships built on endearing engagement and the truth of God's Word. 

CONNECT

We believe living connected to God and others is the best way to find the purpose and joy that makes life meaningful and fulfilling.

CARE

Love is a verb. We take caring for each other and our community seriously. 

CREATE

We help people create the faith, life, marriage, family, workplace, & community they desire and were meant for.

Family Life

Here's a look at some of the ways we engage each other, our community, and the globe...

Sunday Gathering

We gather together as a faith family for
a time of worship and fellowship through
prayer, music, Scripture exploration, giving,
teaching, discussion, sharing the Sacraments, and more. Open to all ages 10:00am weekly.

Connection Group

Our Wednesday night gathering provides
a more intimate small group environment
as we meet in homes to fellowship, share, discuss, pray, laugh, and study the Scriptures together. Great way to build relationships and grow in Christ through spiritual formation.
6:30-8pm 1st & 3rd Wednesday of the month.

Youth Programs

From Jr. & Sr. High Youth Group, to Sunday morning Kid's Time, to our Kid's Club! and VBS, we have various opportunities for
youth to experience a safe and loving environment where they can connect with
others, grow their faith, and have a blast doing it!

Community Food Pantry

We operate a community food pantry
open to all on the 3rd Saturday of each month from 9am-11am based on size of household. This is truly a community endeavor as several other churches, individuals, and organizations
donate regularly in order to prevent
hunger in our area.
